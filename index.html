Our Major Services

Project Planning

Comprehensive planning services including project performance reports, manpower histograms, S-curves, contingency plans, risk management, and Power BI dashboards to ensure projects stay on schedule and budget.

Project Design

Full design solutions from concept to detailed drawings: 2D/3D plans, interior design, structural & MEP plans, approved layouts, stability certificates, shop drawings, technical data, and schematic designs.

Commercial

Financial and commercial support: cash flow analysis, profit/loss reports, project cost tracking, ROI calculations, loan assistance, bank support, and bill preparation for clients/subcontractors.

Initiate

Project startup services: soil investigation, initial surveys, borewell/open well planning, site office setup, temporary utilities, mobilization plans, land assessment, and feasibility studies.

Legal / Close Out

Legal and closure expertise: legal opinions, construction law advice, dispute resolution, completion certificates, close-out strategies, as-built documentation, and commissioning plans.

Contracts / QS

Contract and quantity surveying: agreement drafting, BOQ preparation, material reconciliation, variation analysis, cost efficiency reviews, budget preparation, and cost-benefit analysis.

Execution

On-site execution support: execution plans, construction audits, risk identification, formwork schemes, machinery erection, MEP/civil/structural works, and weekly inspection reports.

Safety

HSE services: safety plans, audits, evacuation/traffic plans, calibration certificates, training/induction, toolbox talks, and statistical safety reporting.

Statutory

Compliance assistance: company/GST registration, permits, labor licenses, practice licenses, CAR/WCP/ESI policies, and green building reports.

Documents

Documentation support: access to IS/BS/US codes, FIDIC/JCT formats, report templates, data analysis, and project document management.

Finance

Financial advisory: REIT guides, real estate investment, debt/equity advisory, marketing (online/offline), government subsidies, and property management.

General

Miscellaneous services: tenant representation, document translation, business plans, rental agreements, compliance expertise, content writing, and website development.

Quality

Quality assurance: defect/snag tracking, audits, corrective actions, work method statements, PERT/Six Sigma analysis, and quality control charts.

Schedule

delay/claim analysis, timeline extensions, Scheduling services: baseline/recovery/tracking programs, milestone/micro/mitigation schedules, monthly/weekly reports, and progress presentations.

Specialized Works

Niche construction: demolition, piling, swimming pools, lifts, waterproofing, ETP/STP/WTP, DG sets, MEP, landscape, and glazing.

Software Tutor

Training in tools: BIM, AutoCAD, Primavera, MS Project, Revit, Navisworks, Planswift, Tekla, simulation, Power BI, and virtual assistance.
